How it All Began

Meet VDJ Shailesh, a renowned international tour DJ hailing from the vibrant city of Mumbai. With over 22 years of experience in the music industry, VDJ Shailesh has become a true maestro behind the decks, captivating audiences worldwide with his electrifying performances.

Having embarked on more than 30 international tours, VDJ Shailesh has left an indelible mark on the global music scene. His incredible talent has been showcased in over 7000 shows, where he seamlessly blends genres, creating an unforgettable musical journey for his audience.
